I am a third-year graduate student in Computer Science at [Peking University](https://english.pku.edu.cn/), advised by Prof. [Yuexian Zou](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=sfyr7zMAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN). Previously, I earned a B.A. in Environmental Engineering from [Sun Yat-sen University](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/sysuen/).

My research interests include enhancing the general capabilities of Vision-Language Models (VLMs) and building AI agents that can truly perceive and interact with the world. Currently working in LongCat Foundation Model team to build **frontier Omni and Vision-Language Model**.
